== Description ==

Turn on mobile notifications for your Wordpress with HookNotifier.

- Monitor what happens on your site in real time
- Receive real notifications on your smartphone when something happens on WP
- Activate or desactivate events to filter your notifications

You'll just have to install HookNotifier app on your smartphone to get your API keys.

This plugin is entirely free.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the HookNotifier plugin to your Wordpress
2. Activate it
3. Click on Settings or go to HookNotifier admin page
4. If you havn't download HookNotifier yet, go to Hooknotifier.com (or on Playstore/AppStore) to get your app
5. Fill your identifiers inside your Wordpress admin
